# 12 Templates

Reusable note templates for the Vault. Copy the relevant template into its target folder rather than editing these in place.

- `ADR Template.md` — for `07 ADR/`
- `Specification Template.md` — for `08 Specifications/`
- `Project Template.md` — for `09 Projects/`
- `SOP Template.md` — for `10 SOP/`
- `Meeting Notes Template.md` — for meeting records (file under the relevant Project, or `11 AI Collaboration/` for AI-collaboration syncs)
